Quality & standards

Engineered to standards your market trusts.

Authenticity is the easy part when you make at origin. Proving it to a regulator and a buyer abroad is what the quality system is built around, with a Certificate of Analysis on every batch.

01Regulatory scope

Facility commissioning to meet the following regulatory scopes. These are the standards the facility is being built and commissioned to, not certificates already held.

Target scope · GMP

WHO-GMP

The global baseline for safe, consistent manufacturing.

Target scope · AYUSH

Internationally recognised food-safety management.

Target scope · Halal

Halal
