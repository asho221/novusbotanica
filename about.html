About

A CDMO built around one advantage: India.

We engineer the supplements other brands sell — pairing the country's botanical sourcing with the formulation science, standards and documentation those brands need to ship anywhere.

Novus Botanica is a contract development and manufacturing organisation. Brands arrive with a formula or a brief and leave with finished, labelled, documented stock, without standing up a facility, a QC lab or an export desk of their own.

The advantage is simple. India grows the botanicals the wellness category runs on, and makes them to standards the US, UK, EU and Gulf recognise. That combination, authentic sourcing plus advanced formulation and global compliance, is the whole reason a brand abroad would manufacture here rather than down the road.

We work at the low MOQs early brands need, build delivery into the formula so claims hold up, and scale as SKUs prove out. The aim is to be the one partner a brand keeps from first pilot to volume.
